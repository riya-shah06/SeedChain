Seed supply chain is a complex ecosystem involving various stakeholders. Seed quality issues have demanded a need for effective traceability solution. Several important criteria such as origin of the seed, stages in production, conformance to quality standards such as genetic purity, germination rate etc. are to be validated. This platform is a solution to all the problem. It is a one stop shop for every stakeholder in the supply chain.
